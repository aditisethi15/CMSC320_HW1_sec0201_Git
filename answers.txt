Q1: Why might we consider using Git?
Answer: Using Git is helpful in order to track our changes, collaborate with others on a project, and edit files from different devices.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.
Answer: Data science could be applied to various different fields, including finance, healthcare, government, manufacturing, etc. 

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?
Answer: Merging combines the histories of the two branches into a single branch, while rebasing rewrites history by putting changes from one branch at the beginning of another.
